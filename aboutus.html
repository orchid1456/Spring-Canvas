ABOUT US

Spring Canvas provides users with various outfits for new and upcoming fashion trends put together by luxury brands. Every season and year, luxury brands come out with a new collection. To help these brands promote their new collection to the public, we want to create a lookbook that specifically targets fashion students, fashionista, influencers, celebrities, and the general public on these upcoming collections. The goal is to successfully market, educate, inform, and develop an appreciation for the collection as a piece of artwork. This lookbook will specifically only present the Spring 2021 Collection brought out by each brand.
